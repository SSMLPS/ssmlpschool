About

S.S.M.L.P.SCHOOL

Seethi Sahib Memmorial Lower Primary school situated in a small village called as Kudamunda in Pallarimangalam Gramapanchayath near the banks of Kothamangalam River. Eight kilometers away from Kothamangalam Town and at the eastern end of Ernakulam District of Kerala State. The school started functioning on the 6 th day of june 1979, in the name of Hononrable Marhum Seethi Sahib under the management of Sri. P.M Muhammed Pokkappillil Kudamunda. Then honourable Education Minister Sri. Muhammed Koya Sahib gave sanction to start a L. P. School at Kudamunda. The school started its functioning in a near by rented building owned by Sri. Moidheen Perumpanchalil as there is no separate school building with in a short period a ‘pacca’ building for the school was built-up with the help of the people of Kudamunda and well wishers .

Sri. M.S Muhammed Ali was appointed as the first Headmaster of the school and three more teachers were appointed in the first instance. One protected teacher was also accommodated in the school. Sri. Muhammed Ali the First Headmaster who retired on super annuation from the school on 31st March 2005 and Smt. Smt. Rosemol Lonappan took charge as Headmistress. Consequent to the retirement of Smt. Rosemol Lonappan , Smt. Jessy C.K took charg as Headmistress on 31st March 2019 as Headmistress and continuing as such. Under the management of sarvasri, P.S Aliyar, P.M Muhammed Kunnju, Varghese Kutty, K.M Pareeth the school become more prosperous and popular among the neighbouring places. Now the school is under the management of well know educational body “Green Vision Educational & Charitable Trust” headed by Sri. P.H Ilyas. Now the school has a fully equipped new building with all kinds of facilities such as smart class rooms, computer lab room etc...... . The staff and management are doing their level best for the proficiency and well being of the students in the school.

VISION

The school was started in 1979 at Kodamunda, Kothamangalam. The school has completed more than 39 years of its service to the normal people of Kodamunda area The Mission of the school is to open the horizon of digital and other forms of knowledge to the people of Kavumakara area and to be top among the schools.

MISSION
