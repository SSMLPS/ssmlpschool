About

S.S.M.L.P.School

Seethi Sahib Memmorial Lower Primary school situated in a small village called as Kudamunda in Pallarimangalam Gramapanchayath near the banks of Kothamangalam River. Eight kilometers away from Kothamangalam Town and at the eastern end of Ernakulam District of Kerala State. The school started functioning on the 6 th day of june 1979, in the name of Hononrable Marhum Seethi Sahib under the management of Sri. P.M Muhammed Pokkappillil Kudamunda. Then honourable Education Minister Sri. Muhammed Koya Sahib gave sanction to start a L. P. School at Kudamunda.......... MORE
